I am a Postdoctoral Associate at Boston University, Questrom School of Business. I received my PhD through a joint doctoral program between the University of Victoria (Canada) and Xi'an Jiaotong University (China). My research lies in healthcare operations management, healthcare AI, AI and workforce, with a focus on how emerging technologies reshape operational performance, patient outcomes, and healthcare workforce.

## Research Interests

- Healthcare operations management
- Healthcare AI
- Healthcare IT
- AI and workforce
