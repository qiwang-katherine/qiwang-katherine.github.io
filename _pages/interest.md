Beyond research, I find a lot of joy in music and dance.

I started learning the **guzheng** (a traditional Chinese plucked string instrument) at the age of six, and earned my Level 10 certification from the China Nationalities Orchestra Society at ten. Over the years, I have given several solo performances. Playing the guzheng has been a quiet companion through long stretches of research, giving me space to slow down between papers.
