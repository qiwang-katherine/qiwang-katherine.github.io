I started learning the **Guzheng** (a classical Chinese plucked string instrument) at the age of six, and earned the highest (Level 10) certification from the China Orchestral Society at ten. I have given several solo performances. The Guzheng has remained a place to find stillness.

I've also been dancing Latin and waltz, and enjoy them in my spare time.
